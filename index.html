Top 3 Options for my Next Car

  • Ford Focus ST
  • Ford Fiesta ST
  • Subaru WRX

Ford Focus ST

The Ford Focus ST is a great choice for a new or used car and is probably my first choice when its time for me to get my next car. The car comes naturally with over 250 bhp and has a ton of aftermarket materials to add to the car. The pros of owning this car are the price for the power, the overall look of the vehicle and the fact that you can mod the car very easily and there are many sites with how to videos on how to do so. The cons are that it is only front wheel drive and its hard to find one with low miles for a low price.

Ford Fiesta ST

The Ford Fiesta ST is another great choice when it comes to me buying my next car. In all honesty it is basically just a smaller version of the Focus ST. The pros to this car are; it is cheaper than the Focus ST and easier to find one with low miles. However, the cons are; it is less powerful than the Focus ST and also doesn't look as nice in my opinion.

Subaru WRX

The Subaru WRX, while not being my first pick when it comes to buying a new car, would be my favorite car of the three to obtain. Its pros are the fact that its all-wheel drive and is slightly more powerful than the Focus ST. However, its cons are its price and fuel economy.
